Introduction

With the rapid advancement of large model technologies and continuous breakthroughs in multimodal capabilities, large model-driven data compression has emerged as a core direction to break through the bottlenecks of traditional compression technologies and lead the transformation of next-generation information processing technologies. It integrates the powerful semantic understanding and prediction capabilities of large models with the coding theory of classical data compression to achieve extreme lossless compression and efficient storage of data. This special track focuses on the cutting-edge intersection of large model technologies and data compression, aiming to gather global academic achievements and industrial practices, explore solutions to the key technical challenges confronting large model-driven data compression, and promote the innovation and application of new-generation data compression technologies across various industries. We warmly invite researchers, engineers and practitioners worldwide to submit original research works.

Topics

  • Fundamental Theories and Paradigms of Large Model-Driven Data Compression
  • Large Language Models for Lossless Compression
  • Multimodal Compression Using Large Models
  • AI-Native Image, Audio and Video Compression
  • Large Models & Security, Privacy and Trustworthy Compression
