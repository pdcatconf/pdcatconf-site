Introduction

The rapid advancement of intelligent systems, coupled with the growing need for scalable computational solutions, has brought Parallel and Distributed Computing (PDC) technologies to the forefront of innovation. This special track aims to explore the convergence of intelligent algorithms, systems, and architectures within PDC technologies to address modern computational challenges. Of particular interest are groundbreaking methods and applications that leverage distributed systems for tasks such as optimization, learning, decision making, and large-scale simulation. The track also welcomes discussions on energy-efficient, adaptive, and sustainable PDC solutions that are fundamental for modern AI-driven applications. We aim to foster collaboration among researchers, developers, and practitioners to exchange ideas and advance the state of the art in intelligent PDC technologies applicable to diverse fields, such as big data processing, IoT, cloud/edge computing, and high-performance computing. Submissions are encouraged from interdisciplinary teams and fields that align with the principles of PDC innovations.

Topics

  • Scalable AI Algorithms and Frameworks for Parallel and Distributed Computing
  • Energy-efficient and Sustainable Strategies in PDC and Cloud/Edge Computing
  • Real-time and Adaptive Systems for Intelligent IoT and Distributed Sensors
  • Applications of PDC in Big Data Analytics, High-Performance Computing, and Real-world AI Systems
